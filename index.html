SRCC Youth Conference, 2016

20-21 September, 2016

Shri Ram College of Commerce, New Delhi.



Youth Conference is a student-to-student initiative whose objective is to stimulate the biggest (and currently latent) strength of an emerging nation, i.e. its youth. It aims to bring together distinguished people, who have carved a niche in their respective fields, to share their milestones, downfalls, struggles and successes, inspiring the aspirants to achieve beyond mediocre boundaries.

Venue

The youth conference will take place in the SRCC auditorium.
