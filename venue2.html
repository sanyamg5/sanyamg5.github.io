Nordic Ruby 2016

June 17-19, Stockholm, Sweden

Venue

Nordic Ruby will take place at Yasuragi Hasseludden — a peaceful oasis where you can enjoy Japanese baths, a relaxing atmosphere in the beautiful Stockholm archipelago, and delicious Japanese and Asian cooking. Yasuragi is designed to provide the ultimate conditions for work and rest.

The conference will be held in the biggest meeting room, Fuji — a large, spacious hall with a unique and dynamic interior, complete with panoramic windows that span seven metres high. You will also have access to lounging areas and a big balcony overlooking the archipelago.

Start your day in the spa area where you can enjoy the Japanese bathing ritual before you continue in the large swimming pool or outdoor hot tubs. Or unwind with the other attendees and speakers in the steam rooms or Nordic saunas.

Arrival

You can arrive at Yasuragi by car, bus or boat. See our blog post on how to get to the venue for more information.

Stockholm

Stockholm is the capital of Sweden and the country’s largest city. It is located in the heart of Scandinavia, on Sweden’s southeast coast, by the Baltic Sea.

The city was founded in the early 13th century, and is renowned for its beauty, clean open water, fascinating history, broad variety of arts and culture.

If you’re staying in town beyond the conference, be sure to check out Gamla Stan, visit some of the city’s multitude of museums, stop by the Royal Palace, try the rides at Gröna Lund amusement park on Djurgården, participate in some roof top hiking on Riddarholmen, or sample some of the city’s more than 1000 restaurants. Whatever you prefer, there’s something for everyone in The City on the Water.
